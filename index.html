Bicol Region also known as Region V is located in the southern part of the island of Luzon in the Philippines. This place has six provinces namely Albay, Camarines Norte, Camarines Sur, Catanduanes, Masbate, and Sorsogon. There are numerous wonderful places in Bicol Region. Some are beautiful because of the vegetations, and natural interventions, Some are historic, and some contains all the qualities that a tourist attraction should have. Explore the breathtaking beauty of Bicol Region and discover its historic culture.
